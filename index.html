About me

I first walked into a recording studio in 1988 when I was 17 years old and I instantly fell in love with the music production process. After 2 years of training I started producing local artists while adapting to the analog to digital technology transition.

A few years later I started teaching at the recording school I went to and discovered that I was equally as passionate about education. I love breaking complex ideas down into easy to understand metaphors and practicing my stand-up comedy on the unsuspecting students.

I have now been a recording and music prodcution instructor for more than 25 years and taught hundreds of students at a number of schools some of whom have gone on to have amazing careers in a multitude of job types and musical genres. Recently I have become one of around only 350 Ableton Certified Trainers worldwide and started my own company called LEVELS.
